SpaceTech: Computing vs. The Solar System

  1. Computing has allowed us to see places far beyond our planet.
  2. Without it, we could never execute the perilous stages of launching rockets into orbit and beyond.
  3. Robotic rovers let us traverse and analyse extraterrestrial vistas. Computing helps design, land and control them.

Computing has enabled us to venture a little further into the vast unknowns of our universe.
