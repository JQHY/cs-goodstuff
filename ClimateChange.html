Climate Change

Improving climate modelling

One of the largest ways that Computer scientist can help climate change is through modelling with data that has been collected. Climate modelling has enabled us to get a better idea of the impact of climate change on the planet. It gives us a more acuate view of:

  • Where? Some places are much more severe affected. It makes it easier to predict where is likely to be next hit by extreme weather such as floods.
  • How? Additional modelling also allows us to predict the greater impact of these changes. We can model the impact on animals and the biodiversity of the planet.
  • When? We are also able to better predict when these things are going to happen enabling us to better prepare for the future.
