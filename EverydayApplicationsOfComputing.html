Everyday applications of Computing

Computing has been leveraged by Computer Scientists as a tool used to tackle problems that we face every day.
From buying a coffee using contactless to using Google Maps to find the fastest route to work; we have become dependent on the application of computing in our daily routines whether we know it or not, arguably for the better.

More recently supermarkets have taken a computational approach to keep track of the number of customers in the store in real-time. This has helped the supermarkets make more accurate decisions on how many more customers they can let into the store at that given-time in line with the government guidelines on Covid-19. This is just one of the many applications of computing for good and Computer Scientists are developing creative solutions to problems just like these that we are faced with everyday. I for one am excited by what computing has to bring for the future.
